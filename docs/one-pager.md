### The Problem

Traditional Product Owners **plan** but don't **build**. They write backlogs, wait for dev teams, and hope things ship. Meanwhile, AI coding agents can build entire products in hours — but POs don't know how to use them.

### The Solution: VelaraLoop

A 6-phase framework that turns Product Owners into **AI-powered builders** who deliver real products, not just slide decks.

---

### The 6 Phases

| Phase | What you do | Output |
|:-----:|-------------|--------|
| 🔍 **DISCOVER** | Research users & problems | Discovery Brief |
| 📋 **DEFINE** | Write specs & prioritize | SoW + Backlog |
| ⚡ **BUILD** | Execute with AI agents | Working code |
| 🚀 **SHIP** | Deploy to production | Live product |
| 📊 **GOVERN** | Track & document | Changelog + history |
| 🔄 **LEARN** | Measure & iterate | Next priorities |

---

### Two Modes

**Solo Mode** — You + AI agents. No dev team needed.
**Team Mode** — You lead devs who use AI. Ship 3-10x faster.
