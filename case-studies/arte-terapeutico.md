## The VelaraLoop in Action

### DISCOVER ✅
- Extensive market research: wellness, art therapy positioning, Lima audience
- Problem identified: professionals need stress relief beyond yoga/meditation
- 5 customer segments mapped (professional women, couples, corporates, wellness community, beginners)
- Competitor landscape: generic paint nights vs. intentional wellness positioning
- Key insight: "Art for calm, not art for art's sake"
- Pricing validated: S/90-220 B2C, S/2,500-8,000 B2B corporate
- Output: 30-page product research document

### DEFINE (next)
- Brand decided: **Calma Studio** (paraguas) + **Charm Colours** (social/viral line)
- 7 products designed:
  1. Calma Art (pintura consciente, S/90-150)
  2. Charm Colours Neon Night (Van Gogh, UV, S/120-180)
  3. Wine & Paint (premium, couples, S/150-220)
  4. Calma Rituals (cacao, matcha, journaling, S/90-140)
  5. Calma Flow (yoga + paint, S/100-160)
  6. Calma Corporate (B2B, from S/2,500)
  7. 21 Days Drawing Program (digital, S/49-199)
- Workshop structure: 7-step facilitation guide
- Unit economics: S/2,400 revenue / ~S/1,650 cost per event (20 pax)
- SoW to be written for MVP execution

### BUILD (planned)
- Landing page (Next.js, deployed via VelaraLoop Solo Mode)
- Instagram + TikTok accounts
- WhatsApp Business
- 3 pilot workshop scripts
- Materials procurement list
- Corporate brochure PDF

### SHIP (planned)
- 3 pilot workshops in Lima (Calma Art + Neon Night + Rituals)
- Landing live with booking (Cal.com) + payments (Mercado Pago)
- First corporate proposal sent

### GOVERN (planned)
- Changelog per workshop (attendance, NPS, revenue, learnings)
- SoW tracking (phases marked as complete)
- Customer database in Attio CRM

### LEARN (planned)
- NPS per event
- Recompra rate
- Content generated per event (reels, photos, testimonials)
- Pricing validation (which ticket sells fastest)
- Corporate pipeline conversion
